Introduction

My name is Harry Wong and I am currently studying design at the School of Interactive Arts and Technology. Growing up, I always had a gravitation toward visuals. I preferred images over words and movies over books. Ultimately, my love for visuals has lead into the realm of user interface design. As I progress through my academic career, I have learned many technical skills, such as the utilization of different softwares, and many soft skills, such as being able to communicate my ideas. All in all, I design because I want to see people interact with my work, and evoke their emotions.
